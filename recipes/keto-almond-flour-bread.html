Almond Flour Bread

For all you ketogenic people out there, this recipe is one of my all-time favourite types of bread. Enjoy!

Ingredient list:

    3 1/2 cups of blanched Almond Flour
    1/4 cup of grass-fed butter (or extra virgin oil)
    210 g of 0 % fat greek yoghurt
    3 eggs
    1/4 tsp of salt
    1/4 cream of tartar

Procedure:

    1. Mix the dry ingredients (i.e.almond flour, salt and cream of tartar) in a plastic recipient.
    2. Mix the wet ingredients (i.e. eggs, greek yoghurt, grass-fed butter) in another plastic recipient.
    3. Mix the wet and dry ingredients.
    4. On a medium-sized steel recipient for bread, spray a little bit of oil and the blended mixture you previously did.
    5. Place the recipient inside of the oven at 180 C for 45-50 minutes.
    6. Enjoy!

EXTRA TIP: Place a knife on top of the bread and take it out. If it comes clean, it means your bread is ready, but if it does not, wait for 10 more minutes.
